Our Legacy & Expertise

Alfa Impex Tanning Industries (AIT Industries) has its roots in a family legacy that dates back to 1952, when it was first established as Skin Suppliers. What began as a modest business exporting dry salted hides soon evolved into a fully integrated tannery specializing in finished leather production. Over the decades, AIT Industries has embraced innovation while staying true to its expertise in sourcing the finest raw leather. Today, the company processes over 300,000 square feet of premium finished leather per month, catering to the demands of some of the most well-known global brands.

With its tannery located in Ranipet, India, AIT Industries has been a trusted supplier to Bata and Tata for over 20 years. The company takes pride in its ability to maintain strict quality control—ensuring that every piece of leather is produced from carefully selected raw hides. By combining traditional craftsmanship with state-of-the-art machinery, AIT Industries delivers technically superior and commercially viable leather that meets international standards. Despite the evolution of the industry, the company’s unwavering commitment to quality and ethical, sustainable tanning practices remains its core strength.
