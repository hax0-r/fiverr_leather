About Us

A Legacy of Leather Craftsmanship

Our Journey: From Skin Suppliers to AIT Industries

Established in 1952, we began as trusted suppliers of raw hides. Over the decades, we evolved into AIT Industries, pioneering modern leather production while maintaining our traditional craftsmanship.

Our Mission & Values

At AIT, we are dedicated to producing high-quality leather through sustainable and ethical sourcing practices. Our focus remains on innovation, craftsmanship, and environmental responsibility.

Leadership & Expertise

Our leadership team brings decades of expertise in raw leather procurement, tanning technology, and global trade. Their commitment ensures precision in every stage of leather production.

Our Facility & Process

Our state-of-the-art tannery combines advanced European machinery with traditional techniques. From raw hide selection to finishing, every step is meticulously controlled for superior quality.
